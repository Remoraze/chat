How to Write a Great Essay

By The Alpha Oil Executive | StudyCraft Blog

Essay writing can be stressful, especially when the topic feels overwhelming or when you're not sure where to begin. But with the right structure and mindset, anyone can craft a compelling and organized essay. This guide walks you through a proven strategy I've personally used throughout high school and university.

1. Understand the Prompt

Always break the question down into smaller parts. Highlight the key verbs and make sure you understand what it's really asking before writing anything.

2. Use the TEAL Paragraph Method

  • Topic Sentence
  • Evidence
  • Analysis
  • Link back to the main argument

This technique keeps your writing clear, persuasive, and on-topic.

3. Review and Refine

Leave time to edit! A good essay is never written in one draft. I recommend printing it out or reading it aloud to catch flow or grammar issues.
